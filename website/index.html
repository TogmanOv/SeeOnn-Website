Eye examination in SeeOnn

A prophylactic eye examination can detect the causes of headaches,
high blood pressure, glaucoma and cataracts, keratoconus, or even
the presence of tumors and incipient diabetes. We control the
parameters of vision using the most modern devices, which prevents
the appearance of more serious complications!
Anamnesis (remember), that is, the totality of data on the state of health of the examined person. The goal is to acquire the most
comprehensive picture of health problems that the subject has been
treated in the past or is currently undergoing treatment.
